GorillaX by Naufr4g0
====================

Platform: Commodore64
Language: Microsoft BASIC V2

Description:
This is a classic game for two players.
Your goal is to hit your opponent with a banana using the two variables
at your disposal: angle (ANG) and force (STR) of the shot.
The player who first scores 3 points wins.

Instructions:
Players use alternately the keyboard to input shot values.
Look at the attached image for more details.
